Sunflowers are one of the fastest growing plants and they grow tall. On an average they grow up to 8-12 feet within six months.
A single sunflower can have up to 2000 seeds.
While a few sunflower varieties do not need any staking, it is a good idea to support plants that grow over 3 feet tall or are multi-branched.
It takes 7-10 days for a seed to sprout, and 80-120 days to reach maturity.
Sunflowers do not require fertilizer, unless planted in nutrient-poor, thin soil.
